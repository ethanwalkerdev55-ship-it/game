DisUnityFF (OpenFusion fork)
============================

Version base: DisUnity 0.3.4
Fork: OpenFusionProject/disunityff
Built from: tools/downloads/OpenFusion-disunityff (2026-06-06)

This is the FusionFall / OpenFusion modding build. It is NOT:
- stock ata4/disunity 0.3.4 (backup: disunity-stock-ata4-v0.3.4.jar.bak)
- ata4/disunity 0.5.x (different CLI: bundle-unpack / bundle-pack only)

Extra command vs stock 0.3.4:
- bundle-create  — pack folders into bundles (optional)

Standard FusionFall workflow:
  disunity.bat bundle-extract <path>\main.unity3d
  (edit DLL in dnSpy)
  disunity.bat bundle-inject <path>\main.unity3d

Verify install:
  disunity.bat bundle-list <path>\main.unity3d

You should see bundle-create in:
  disunity.bat -h
